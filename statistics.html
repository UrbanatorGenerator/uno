Privacy Policy for Powersales Communication

Your personal privacy matters to us.

What is GDPR?

GDPR is a European Union regulation that governs how companies and organizations collect and use personal data — that is, any information that can identify an individual, such as a name, phone number, or address.

What information do we collect?

We collect the following information through our contact form in order to respond to inquiries, provide information, and offer our services to you.

  • Name: To know who we are communicating with.
  • Email address: To be able to reach you via email.
  • Phone number: To make it easier to get in touch with you.
  • Message: To assist you and address your questions.

Your personal data is stored securely, with access strictly limited to authorized personnel within Powersales Communication. Information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the stated purpose.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or device when you visit certain websites. These files typically contain information about your browsing activity and are used by websites to remember user preferences, authenticate users, and gather analytics.

We do not use cookies. Powersales Communication is committed to your privacy and wants to be transparent: we do not use any cookies on our website. We are dedicated to providing a straightforward and transparent online experience — free from tracking or the storage of personal information via cookies.

What are your rights?

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. If you wish to submit such a request, or if you have any questions regarding how your personal data is handled, please do not hesitate to contact us.

We encourage you to reach out if you have any further questions about our data practices. Our goal is to support you and provide the information you need. info@powersales.se

About this chatbot

Data we collect: Chat messages are processed by Anthropic's Claude API and temporarily stored in session memory (Redis). Sessions expire automatically.

Usage analytics: Anonymised usage statistics (message count, token usage, cost estimates) are logged to a private Google Sheet for operational purposes.

Market data: Financial data sourced from Yahoo Finance and Open Exchange Rates, delayed ~15 minutes.

No personal data is sold or shared with third parties. Questions? info@powersales.se

📈 What is this dashboard?

This is a statistics dashboard for an AI chatbot. It shows how much the chatbot is being used and what it costs to run it — updated in real time.

Conversations are simply the number of times a user has started a chat with the bot. More conversations means more people are using it.

Tokens are how AI measures text. Every word (or part of a word) you type, and every word the AI responds with, counts as tokens. The more you chat, the more tokens are used — and tokens cost money.

Cost is directly tied to tokens. The company that provides the AI (in this case Anthropic/Claude) charges per token, so the cost goes up the more people use the chatbot.

The bar chart shows how the daily cost has changed over the past two weeks. You can spot busy days where costs spike higher than normal.

The donut chart shows which platforms people use to talk to the chatbot — for example via a website chat or through Telegram (a messaging app).

Total cost is the running bill since the chatbot was first launched — a useful number for whoever is paying for it to keep track of the overall spend.

In short: this dashboard gives the company running the chatbot a quick overview of usage, costs, and where their users are coming from.
